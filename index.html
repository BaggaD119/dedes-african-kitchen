About Us

Home-cooked African food, made with love

Dede's African Kitchen started with a simple idea: bring the taste of real, home-cooked African food to anyone craving it. Every dish is made fresh to order, in small batches, using the same recipes and care you'd find across kitchens from Ghana to the rest of the continent.

No shortcuts, no mass prep — just Jollof, Waakye, and more of the continent's favorites, cooked the right way and delivered hot to your door.
