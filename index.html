About Us

Home-cooked Ghanaian food, made with love

Dede's African Kitchen started with a simple idea: bring the taste of real, home-cooked Ghanaian food to anyone craving it. Every dish is made fresh to order, in small batches, using the same recipes and care you'd find in a Ghanaian home kitchen.

No shortcuts, no mass prep — just Jollof, Waakye, and more, cooked the right way and delivered hot to your door.

FAQ

Common Questions

Everything you need to know before you order.

Do I need an account to order?

No — you can browse the full menu and place an order right away. Creating an account is optional and only useful if you want your order history saved across visits.

How long does delivery take?

Most orders arrive in 15–30 minutes, depending on how busy the kitchen is and your distance from us.

Can I pick up my order instead of delivery?

Yes — choose Pickup instead of Delivery at checkout and we'll have it ready for you at the kitchen.

How do I pay for my order?

After you place an order online, we'll call or message you to confirm the order and arrange payment. You can also call us directly at +1 (519) 477-4701 to order and pay by phone.

Can I change or cancel an order after placing it?

Call us as soon as possible at +1 (519) 477-4701 — we'll do our best to update or cancel it before the kitchen starts preparing your food.
